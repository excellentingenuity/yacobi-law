Family Portfolios

The Yacobi Law Firm encourages adoptive clients to create a portfolio featuring an introductory letter and family photos that birth mothers can review. This portfolio helps the prospective birth mother select the family she feels will be most suitable for her newborn.

Adoptive families now have the option of posting their portfolios on the Internet through our website, thus making it available to a wider number of prospective birth mothers. Portfolios posted on our site are not visible to the general public; only prospective birth mothers can view your materials.

If you have a family portfolio already assembled or in process, and you wish to have it published on this website, please call us or send an e-mail us. If you already have a personal web page, feel free to post your portfolio there, and we will provide a link.
