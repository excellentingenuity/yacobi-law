Who We Are

For three decades, Stephen Yacobi has dedicated himself to creating happier families through the practice of law. Since graduating with honors from Wofford College and the University of South Carolina School of Law, he has helped place hundreds of children in stable, loving homes. He has been a member of the South Carolina Bar since 1980 and a Fellow of the American Academy of Adoption Attorneys since 1993.



What We Do

We are a general practice firm in Greenville, S.C., with a passion for Adoption and Family Law. We handle domestic, foreign, interstate and step-parent adoptions. We coordinate independent adoptions as well as agency adoptions, and we have experience in adoptions involving Assisted Reproductive Technology.


With our deep experience and broad contacts, we can coordinate comprehensive services from outside agencies and professionals, helping to ensure the best possible outcome for all parties.

What We Believe

We believe the adoption process is something to be celebrated – not merely tolerated. We believe that every mother deserves respect and every child deserves a loving family. We believe in finding solutions and providing fresh starts.

We believe that what we’re doing makes a difference.

Whatever your circumstances, we look forward to making a difference for you.
