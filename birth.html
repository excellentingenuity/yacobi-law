Birth Parents

At the Yacobi Law Firm, we understand the difficult choices that you’re facing. You’re bringing a new life into the world, you want the very best for your baby – and you know in your heart that you simply can’t provide it.

We believe that adoption is a loving, selfless choice, and we are committed to helping you every step of the way.

We’ll help you survey your options, understand your rights, and find the loving home that’s just right for your baby. We’ll arrange face-to-face meetings if you prefer, or help you choose your adoptive family through a process that’s completely confidential.

Working with other agencies and professionals, we’ll help you find the support that you need for starting not just one new life, but two.

To learn more, please click on the Frequently Asked Questions above, or simply contact us for a confidential, no-obligation consultation.
