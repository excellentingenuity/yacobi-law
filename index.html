A Not so Perfect World

In a perfect world, your baby would come along when the time is right and the circumstances are ideal.

In the real world, however, things are more complicated. You’re not ready, you’re too young, you can’t provide.

But you do care, and that’s why you’re considering adoption.


We Care Too

We care too, and we’re here to help. Since 1980 the Yacobi Law Firm has been performing a vital role: helping birth parents find a loving, supportive home for their child, while at the same time helping adoptive parents find the child who will complete their families and answer their prayers.

We believe the adoption process should maintain the dignity of both sets of parents, while focusing on the best interests of the child. We aim to make all parties feel confident in the choices they are making for themselves and the future happiness of the child.
